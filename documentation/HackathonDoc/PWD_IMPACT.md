## ğŸ† The "ACCESS.AI" Difference

Most accessible apps stop at **"Compliance"** (making it *possible* to use).
ACCESS.AI aims for **"Empowerment"** (making it *easier* and *faster* to use than for a non-disabled person).

-   **Compliance**: "The blind user can read the document if they spend 30 minutes with a screen reader."
-   **ACCESS.AI**: "The blind user asks a question and gets the answer in 3 seconds."
