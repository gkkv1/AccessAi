**The Problem:** 
Standard captions are often a "wall of text" without speaker attribution. A Deaf user sees: *"I agree... No I don't... Wait, sure."* They cannot tell *who* agreed or disagreed, making active participation impossible.

**The ACCESS.AI Solution (Diarization):**
*   **Mechanism:** The Transcription Engine distinguishes audio fingerprints.
*   **Result:** Transcript shows:

    *   *Sarah:* "No I don't."
*   **Impact:** **95% Accuracy** in speaker identification (powered by Whisper/Pyannote), transforming a passive viewing experience into an active, equitable meeting presence.
